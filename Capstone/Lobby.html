Meet Our Team

Our team is composed of individuals with interest in the field of journalism. We strive to not only provide legitimate and informative articles but also to provide educational materials and the promotion of research works conducted by MUIDS students.

As our motto stated, we stand firm in the meaning of intelligence, integrity and innovative. MUIDS Press boundaries are not only in the school area but also outside in the real world where we can explore and aquire information regarding the well-being of our society.
